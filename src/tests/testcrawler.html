Translation team for Catalan

The Catalan translation team uses ca as its language code. This code is part of the Catalan PO file names. It is also sometimes used as a short identification for the team.

The team uses ca@dodds.net as official email address, which reaches either a mailing list or someone who broadcasts information to all other team members. Jordi Mallach Pérez currently acts as the team leader, and you may write to him or her for all matters related to team coordination.

You can get more information about the Catalan effort here:

Topic URL
Team Site http://ca.dodds.net/
